Our Features

Gate

Manage materials and Visitor movement through your premises. Keep a tab on on-site delegates, material inward-outward, Sales-Purchase returns, Jobwork items and, so much more

Ledgers

Keep a tab on the most critical aspects of business accounting. Generate P&L reports, Balance sheets, Bank & Cash Books, Ledgers, Transactions & MIS reports in real time.

Purchase

Manage key relationships with your procurement stakeholders, map price trends and classify materials on the basis of thier approval statuses. Let's empower your procurement team.

GRN

Maintain your material receipts in an audit-friendly manner. Generate Bar-code markings for your inward items and allocate attributes to them for easy identification and storage.

Invoicing

As an enterprise, maintain a robust Quotation- Proforma Invoice- Invoice Life-cycle. Have the flexibility to effectively map the payment statuses of the invoices for better business.

Inventory

Maintain complex inventory structures in various branches, warehouses and godowns. Generate reports in real time for Stocks, Shelf life, damaged goods and WIP stocks.

Dispatch

Manage your dispatch life cycle by effectively empowering your workforce with packaging, dispatch and associate parameters. Map multi-unit/branch data centrally.

Trading

You buy, you sell! We all do well. Manage Baseline Trading for an MIS use case to effectively maintain your profitability on each and every item on your fingertips.

Reports

Generate a range of basic to advance reports which can be customized and exported in multiple formats for further work and analytics to strengthen your business.
